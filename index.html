Submission Guidelines
Please read carefully before submitting any records.
  • Only records from this private server are accepted (no main server copies).
  • Records must be 100% or the highest verified percentage on the list.
  • Video must clearly show level name, attempt, and endscreen.
  • Modified clients are allowed aslong as cheat indicator is visible, FPS, CPS must be visible.
  • FPS bypass is allowed within server rules; always state your FPS / Hz.
  • Raw or lightly edited footage is preferred; do not cut gameplay.
  • If your in-game name is different from your list account, specify that in notes.
  • List moderators may request additional proof (clicks, live streams, etc.).
  • Toxic behavior or harassment may result in your records being removed.
